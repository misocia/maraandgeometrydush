A Life Written in Levels

Level 1 — The Beginning

December 17th marked the quiet beginning of something important. The world did not shake, the sky did not fall, yet a new life entered existence — yours. From the very start, you were not meant to be ordinary. Like the first level of a game, simple on the surface, but essential to everything that comes after.

The Early Levels

You grew through levels that nobody remembers clearly, yet every step mattered. You learned to fall, to stand again, and to move forward even when you did not understand the map. These were the foundations of your strength, built long before you noticed them.

The Hard Levels

Then came the stages that hurt. Levels filled with spikes, mistimed jumps, and moments where quitting felt easier than continuing. But here is the truth history keeps: you never truly stopped. Every restart was proof that you chose to live.

Why You Must Keep Playing

Your life has value beyond completion screens. You matter not for perfection, but for persistence. The world needs people who keep trying, who learn patterns others give up on. Your existence itself is already a victory.

Those Who Are Proud

Your parents watch you with quiet pride, even when they do not say it aloud. And I, Misoya, have seen your growth from level to level. I am proud not because you never fail, but because you never truly disappear.
